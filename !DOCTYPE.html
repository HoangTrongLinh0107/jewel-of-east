What is The Jewel of the East?

"The Jewel of the East" is a title often attributed to Dammam, a thriving city on the eastern coast of Saudi Arabia. This name reflects its economic vitality, beautiful coastal scenery, and cultural richness.

General Information about Dammam

Key Features of The Jewel of the East:

  • Economic Hub: Dammam is one of the most important industrial and commercial centers in Saudi Arabia.
  • Beautiful Corniche: The city boasts a stunning coastline along the Arabian Gulf, attracting visitors year-round.
  • Cultural Heritage: Home to traditional markets, museums, and historical landmarks that showcase its rich past.

Education in Dammam

It is home to Imam Abdulrahman Bin Faisal University, a prestigious institution known for its contributions to higher education and research in Saudi Arabia.

Top Universities in the Eastern Province:

  1. Imam Abdulrahman Bin Faisal University
  2. King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals
  3. Prince Mohammad Bin Fahd University
  4. University of Dammam
